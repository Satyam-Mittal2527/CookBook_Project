Pad Thai Recipe

Enjoy the classic taste of Thailand with this delicious Pad Thai recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 200g shrimp or tofu
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g bean sprouts
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 lime (for garnish)
  • Crushed peanuts
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water until soft, then drain.
  2. In a pan, saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp or tofu and cook until done.
  4. Push everything to one side of the pan and scramble the eggs on the other side.
  5. Mix in noodles, fish sauce, tamarind paste, and sugar.
  6. Stir in bean sprouts and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with crushed peanuts, lime wedges, and cilantro.
