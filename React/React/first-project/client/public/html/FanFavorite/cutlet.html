Fried Cutlets!

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams ground meat (chicken, beef, lamb, or your choice)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • Oil for frying
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

1
In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, minced ginger, and green chili (if using).
2
Add garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
3
Form the mixture into small patties or cutlets.
4
Dip each cutlet into the beaten egg, then coat with bread crumbs.
5
Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.
6
Fry the cutlets in batches until they are golden brown and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes per side.
7
Remove the cutlets from the pan and drain on paper towels.
8
Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.
