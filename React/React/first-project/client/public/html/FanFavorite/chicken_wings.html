Chicken Wings!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce (your choice)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

1
Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with aluminum foil or parchment paper.
2
In a large bowl, toss the chicken wings with olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
3
Arrange the wings in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.
4
Bake the wings in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, flipping them halfway through, until they are golden brown and crispy.
5
While the wings are baking, melt the butter in a small saucepan over low heat and stir in the hot sauce until well combined.
6
Once the wings are done baking, remove them from the oven and transfer to a large mixing bowl.
7
Pour the butter and hot sauce mixture over the wings and toss to coat evenly.
8
Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
