Pad Thai Noodles!

Ingredients:

Noodles:

  • 200 grams rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 chicken breast, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, chopped
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Pad Thai Sauce:

  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)

Instructions:

1
Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2
In a small bowl, mix all the Pad Thai sauce ingredients until well combined. Set aside.
3
Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
4
Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
5
Add the sliced chicken breast and cook until fully cooked through.
6
Push the chicken to one side of the skillet, then pour the beaten eggs into the empty side and scramble them until fully cooked.
7
Add the cooked rice noodles to the skillet and pour the Pad Thai sauce over the noodles.
8
Toss everything together until well combined and heated through.
9
Add the bean sprouts, julienned carrot, red bell pepper, and chopped green onions. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
10
Remove from heat and garnish with chopped roasted peanuts and fresh cilantro.
11
Serve hot with lime wedges on the side.
