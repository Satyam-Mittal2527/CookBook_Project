Green Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 eggplant, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • Handful of Thai basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan and fry green curry paste for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken slices and stir-fry until browned.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add eggplant, red bell pepper, kaffir lime leaves, fish sauce, and sugar.
  5. Cook for 10-15 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  6. Stir in Thai basil leaves before serving.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
