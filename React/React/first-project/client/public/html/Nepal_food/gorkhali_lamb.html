Gorkhali Lamb Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Gorkhali Lamb:

  • 500g lamb, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Gorkhali Lamb:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee and add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  3. Add the lamb pieces and cook until they are browned and cooked through. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  4. Add garam masala and mix well. Cook for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
