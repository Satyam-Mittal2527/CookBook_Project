Nepali Bhuttan Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Nepali Bhuttan:

  • 500g goat intestines (cleaned and boiled)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Bhuttan:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil and add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and asafoetida. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and mushy.
  4. Add the boiled goat intestines and mix well. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Continue to cook until the intestines are crispy and well-coated with the spices.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
