Chatamari Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Chatamari:

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp salt


    • For Topping:

  • 200g minced meat (chicken, lamb, or pork)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Batter:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rice flour, water, and salt. Mix well to form a smooth batter. The consistency should be similar to pancake batter.
  2. Let the batter rest for about 15-20 minutes.

    ⤷Prepare the Topping:

  1. In a pan, heat oil and add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
  2. Add the minced meat, turmeric powder, cumin powder, and salt. Cook until the meat is browned and cooked through.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft. Set the topping mixture aside.

    ⤷Cook the Chatamari:

  1. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the pan and spread it evenly to form a thin pancake.
  3. Cook the pancake for a few minutes until the bottom is set.
  4. Spread the prepared topping mixture evenly over the pancake.
  5. Sprinkle grated cheese over the topping and cover the pan with a lid.
  6. Cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until the cheese melts and the Chatamari is cooked through.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
