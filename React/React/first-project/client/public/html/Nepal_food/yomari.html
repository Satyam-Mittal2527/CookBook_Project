Yomari Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Yomari Dough:

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt


    • For Filling:

  • 1 cup jaggery (or brown sugar)
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 cup roasted sesame seeds
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Dough:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rice flour and salt.
  2. Gradually add hot water to the rice flour, mixing continuously, until a smooth and pliable dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for a few minutes, then cover it with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 30 minutes.

    ⤷Prepare the Filling:

  1. In a pan, melt the jaggery (or brown sugar) over low heat.
  2. Add the grated coconut, roasted sesame seeds, and ground cardamom to the melted jaggery.
  3. Mix well and cook for a few minutes until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat and let it cool.

    ⤷Shape and Fill the Yomari:

  1. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a ball.
  2. Flatten each ball and shape it into a cone, creating a hollow center for the filling.
  3. Fill the hollow center with the jaggery-coconut mixture.
  4. Seal the open end of the cone by pinching the dough together.

    ⤷Steam the Yomari:

  1. Place the filled Yomari in a steamer and steam for about 15-20 minutes, or until the dough is cooked through and the Yomari are firm.
