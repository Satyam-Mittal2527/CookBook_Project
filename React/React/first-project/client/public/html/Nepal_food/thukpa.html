Thukpa Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Thukpa:

  • 200g noodles (rice noodles or wheat noodles)
  • 250g chicken or vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, etc.), sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chili sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 liter chicken or vegetable broth
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Soup Base:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil and add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  3. Add the sliced chicken or vegetables and cook until they are browned and cooked through.
  4. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.

    ⤷Cook the Noodles:

  1. In a separate pot, cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

    ⤷Combine and Finish:

  1. Add the cooked noodles to the soup base and mix well.
  2. Stir in the soy sauce, chili sauce, and vinegar. Adjust the seasoning to taste.
  3. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
