Kheer Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Nepali Kheer:

  • 1 cup rice (preferably short-grain or Basmati)
  • 1 liter whole milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut (optional, for flavor)
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios)
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp saffron strands (optional, for color)
  • 2 tbsp ghee or clarified butter
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Rice:

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly under cold water and drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat the ghee or clarified butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the rinsed rice and sauté for a few minutes until the rice is lightly toasted.

    ⤷Cook the Kheer:

  1. Pour the milk into the pot with the toasted rice and bring it to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat and let it simmer, stirring occasionally to prevent the rice from sticking to the bottom.
  3. Add the sugar, grated coconut (if using), and a pinch of salt. Mix well.
  4. Continue to simmer the kheer for about 30-40 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked and the mixture thickens to a creamy consistency.
  5. Stir in the chopped nuts, raisins, ground cardamom, and saffron strands (if using). Mix well and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
