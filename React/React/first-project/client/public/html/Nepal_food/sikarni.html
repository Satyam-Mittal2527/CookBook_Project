Sikarni Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Sikarni:

  • 2 cups yogurt (preferably thick and creamy)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios)
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp saffron strands (optional, for flavor and color)
  • 1 tbsp ghee or clarified butter (optional, for flavor)

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Sikarni:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the yogurt and sugar. Mix well until the sugar is fully dissolved.
  2. Add the ground cardamom and saffron strands (if using) to the yogurt mixture. Mix well.
  3. In a small pan, heat the ghee or clarified butter (if using) and lightly toast the chopped nuts and raisins until they are fragrant and slightly golden.
  4. Add the toasted nuts and raisins to the yogurt mixture and mix well.
  5. Transfer the Sikarni to a serving dish and let it chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
