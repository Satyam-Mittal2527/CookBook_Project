Sel Roti Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Sel Roti:

  • 2 cups rice (soaked overnight)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup ghee or vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 ripe banana (optional, for flavor)
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom (optional, for flavor)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Batter:

  1. Rinse the soaked rice thoroughly and drain the water.
  2. In a blender, add the soaked rice, sugar, ghee or vegetable oil, milk, banana (if using), and ground cardamom (if using).
  3. Blend the mixture into a smooth batter. The consistency should be similar to pancake batter. If needed, add a little water to achieve the right consistency.
  4. Pour the batter into a large bowl and let it rest for about 30 minutes.

    ⤷Fry the Sel Roti:

  1. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. The oil should be hot but not smoking.
  2. Pour the batter into a piping bag or a squeeze bottle with a wide nozzle.
  3. Squeeze the batter in a circular motion into the hot oil to form ring shapes.
  4. Fry the Sel Roti until they turn golden brown and crispy on both sides, flipping them as needed.
  5. Use a slotted spoon to remove the Sel Roti from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
