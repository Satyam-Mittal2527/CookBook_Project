Chhang Recipe!

Ingredients

    For Chhang:

  • 1 kg millet or barley
  • 5 liters of water
  • 1-2 tbsp yeast (or traditional starter called "marcha")

Instructions

    ⤷Prepare the Millet/Barley:

  1. Rinse the millet or barley thoroughly under cold water and drain.
  2. In a large pot, bring 5 liters of water to a boil.
  3. Add the millet or barley to the boiling water and cook until they are soft and fully cooked.

    ⤷Ferment the Mixture:

  1. Once the millet or barley is cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. Transfer the cooked millet or barley to a large fermentation vessel.
  3. Add the yeast or traditional starter (marcha) to the mixture and mix well.
  4. Cover the vessel with a cloth or lid and place it in a warm, undisturbed area for fermentation.
  5. Let the mixture ferment for about 5-7 days, stirring occasionally to ensure even fermentation.

    ⤷Strain and Serve:

  1. After the fermentation period, strain the liquid through a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth to separate the solids from the liquid.
  2. The strained liquid is your Chhang, ready to be enjoyed!
