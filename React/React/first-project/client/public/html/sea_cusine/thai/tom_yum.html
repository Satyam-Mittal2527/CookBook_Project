Tom Yum Soup Recipe

Spicy and sour shrimp soup with lemongrass and lime leaves.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 200g shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 3 stalks lemongrass (cut into 2-inch pieces)
  • 4 kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili paste
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 200g mushrooms (sliced)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Bring the broth to a boil in a large pot.
  2. Add lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and chili paste.
  3. Simmer for 5 minutes to release flavors.
  4. Add shrimp and mushrooms, cooking until shrimp turns pink.
  5. Stir in fish sauce and lime juice.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.
