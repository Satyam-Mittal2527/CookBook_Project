Nasi Lemak Recipe

A fragrant Malaysian coconut rice dish served with spicy sambal, crispy anchovies, peanuts, and boiled egg.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 pandan leaves (knotted)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs (boiled)
  • 1/2 cup fried anchovies
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • Sambal sauce (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, combine rice, coconut milk, water, pandan leaves, and salt.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until rice is cooked.
  4. Remove pandan leaves and fluff rice with a fork.
  5. Serve with boiled eggs, fried anchovies, peanuts, cucumber slices, and sambal sauce.
  6. Enjoy this classic Malaysian dish!
