Satay Recipe

Malaysian-style grilled skewered meat served with a rich peanut sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken or beef (cut into thin strips)
  • 10-12 bamboo skewers (soaked in water)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp lemongrass (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup peanut sauce (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, lemongrass, turmeric, coriander, sugar, garlic, and oil.
  2. Add the meat strips and marinate for at least 2 hours.
  3. Thread the meat onto skewers.
  4. Grill over medium heat for 2-3 minutes per side until cooked.
  5. Serve hot with peanut sauce.
