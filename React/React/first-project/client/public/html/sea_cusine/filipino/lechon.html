Lechon Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole pig (small, cleaned)
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 10 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • Banana leaves (for wrapping)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the pig with soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, salt, and pepper.
  2. Let it sit overnight.
  3. Preheat an oven or grill.
  4. Wrap the pig in banana leaves and roast for 4-5 hours, turning occasionally.
  5. Remove banana leaves and roast until crispy.
  6. Serve with lechon sauce.
