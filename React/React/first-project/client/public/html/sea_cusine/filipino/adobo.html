Adobo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken or pork, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, combine meat, soy sauce, vinegar, water, garlic, pepper, and bay leaves.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes.
  3. Remove the lid and let the sauce reduce until thickened.
  4. Serve hot with steamed rice.
