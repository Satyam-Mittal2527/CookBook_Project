Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly or shrimp
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 pack tamarind soup base
  • 1 cup radish, sliced
  • 1 cup kangkong (water spinach)
  • 2 green chili peppers
  • Salt and fish sauce to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, boil pork with water until tender.
  2. Add onions, tomatoes, and tamarind soup base.
  3. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add radish and green chili, then cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Add kangkong last, then season with salt and fish sauce.
  6. Serve hot with rice.
