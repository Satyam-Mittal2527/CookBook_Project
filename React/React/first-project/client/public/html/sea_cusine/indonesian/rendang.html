Rendang Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef, cut into chunks
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 4 kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 lemongrass stalks, bruised
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp chili paste
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt & sugar to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan and saut√© onions, garlic, and chili paste until fragrant.
  2. Add beef and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and add lemongrass, lime leaves, cinnamon, turmeric, and coriander.
  4. Let it simmer on low heat for 3-4 hours until the beef is tender and the sauce thickens.
  5. Stir occasionally to prevent burning.
  6. Serve hot with rice.
