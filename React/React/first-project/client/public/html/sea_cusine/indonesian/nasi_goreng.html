Nasi Goreng Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 200g chicken or shrimp
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
  • 1 tsp chili paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions and garlic.
  2. Add chicken/shrimp and cook until done.
  3. Push everything to one side of the pan and scramble the eggs on the other side.
  4. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, kecap manis, and chili paste.
  5. Stir well and mix in carrots and peas.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  7. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
