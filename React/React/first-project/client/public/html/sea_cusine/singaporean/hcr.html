Hainanese Chicken Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the chicken in water with ginger and garlic until fully cooked.
  2. Remove the chicken and brush with sesame oil.
  3. Cook the rice using chicken broth for added flavor.
  4. Serve with soy sauce and chili sauce on the side.
