Laksa

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp laksa paste
  • 100g shrimp
  • 1 boiled egg (sliced)
  • 100g tofu puffs
  • 2 stalks lemongrass (smashed)

Instructions

  1. Boil water and cook rice noodles, then set aside.
  2. Fry laksa paste with lemongrass until fragrant.
  3. Add coconut milk and simmer.
  4. Add shrimp and tofu puffs, cooking until shrimp is pink.
  5. Pour soup over noodles and top with boiled egg.
