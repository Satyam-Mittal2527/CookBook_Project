Chilli Crab

Ingredients

  • 2 whole crabs (cleaned and cracked)
  • 3 tbsp chili paste
  • 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger (minced)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Fry garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chili paste, tomato sauce, and sugar.
  3. Add crabs and cook until fully coated in sauce.
  4. Serve hot with steamed rice or mantou buns.
