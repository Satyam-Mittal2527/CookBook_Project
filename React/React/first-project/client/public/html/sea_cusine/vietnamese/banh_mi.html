Bánh Mì Recipe

A delicious Vietnamese sandwich with flavorful fillings and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • 200g grilled pork or chicken
  • 1 small carrot, julienned
  • 1 small daikon radish, julienned
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sriracha (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Thinly sliced cucumber
  • Sliced jalapeño (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and a pinch of salt. Add carrots and daikon, and let them pickle for 15 minutes.
  2. Slice the baguette lengthwise and spread mayonnaise on one side.
  3. Add grilled pork or chicken, then drizzle with soy sauce.
  4. Top with pickled carrots and daikon, cucumber slices, jalapeño, and cilantro.
  5. Close the sandwich, press gently, and enjoy!
