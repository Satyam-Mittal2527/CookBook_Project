Pho Recipe

Vietnamese noodle soup with rich broth, beef, and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef bones
  • 500g beef brisket
  • 1 onion, charred
  • 1 piece ginger, charred
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 200g rice noodles
  • 200g thinly sliced beef
  • Fresh bean sprouts
  • Fresh basil and cilantro
  • Lime wedges
  • Sliced chili (optional)

Instructions

  1. Roast bones in the oven at 200Â°C for 30 minutes.
  2. Transfer bones to a large pot and cover with water.
  3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 4-6 hours.
  4. Add charred onion, ginger, star anise, cinnamon, and cloves.
  5. Strain the broth and season with fish sauce.
  6. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions.
  7. Place noodles and raw sliced beef in bowls.
  8. Pour hot broth over the beef to cook it.
  9. Garnish with bean sprouts, basil, cilantro, lime, and chili.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
