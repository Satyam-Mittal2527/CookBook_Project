Crispy Mozzarella Sticks!

Ingredients:

  • 12 mozzarella sticks
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups bread crumbs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Marinara sauce for dipping

Instructions:

1
Prepare the Ingredients: Cut the mozzarella sticks in half if they are too long.
2
Set Up Breading Station: Place the flour, beaten eggs, and bread crumbs in three separate bowls. Season the bread crumbs with garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
3
Bread the Mozzarella Sticks: Dip each mozzarella stick first in flour, then in the beaten eggs, and finally in the seasoned bread crumbs. Press the bread crumbs onto the sticks to ensure they are well-coated.
4
Chill the Sticks: Place the breaded mozzarella sticks on a baking sheet and freeze for at least 1 hour to prevent them from melting too quickly when frying.
5
Heat the Oil: In a deep fryer or large pot, heat oil to 350°F (175°C).
6
Fry the Mozzarella Sticks: Fry the chilled mozzarella sticks in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes. Drain on paper towels.
7
Serve and Enjoy: Serve the mozzarella sticks hot with marinara sauce for dipping.
