Burger Recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams ground meat (your choice: beef, chicken, turkey, or veggie)
  • 1 egg (optional, for binding)
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs (optional, for binding)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles (optional toppings)
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo (optional condiments)

Instructions:

1
Prepare the Meat: In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. If using, add the egg and breadcrumbs for binding.
2
Form the Patties: Divide the meat mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty. Make a small indentation in the center of each patty to prevent it from puffing up during cooking.
3
Preheat the Grill or Pan: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat or heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
4
Cook the Patties: Place the patties on the grill or skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 160째F (71째C) for beef, 165째F (74째C) for chicken or turkey, or according to your preference for veggie patties.
5
Toast the Buns: While the patties are cooking, toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
6
Assemble the Burgers: Place a cooked patty on the bottom half of each bun. Add cheese slices if desired. Top with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments of your choice. Cover with the top half of the bun.
7
Serve and Enjoy: Serve the burgers with your favorite side dishes, such as fries or a salad. Enjoy your delicious homemade burgers!
