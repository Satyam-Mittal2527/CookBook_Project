Delicious Hotdog Delight!

Ingredients:

  • 4 hotdog buns
  • 4 hotdog sausages
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • 1/4 cup relish
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup mustard
  • Optional: sauerkraut, jalapeños, or pickles for garnish

Instructions:

1
Cook the Hotdog Sausages: Grill or boil the sausages until fully cooked.
2
Prepare the Buns: Toast the hotdog buns on the grill or in a toaster until lightly browned.
3
Assemble the Hotdogs: Place a cooked sausage in each bun.
4
Add Toppings: Sprinkle diced onions and shredded cheese over the sausages.
5
Drizzle with ketchup and mustard.
6
Optional: Add sauerkraut, jalapeños, or pickles for extra flavor.
