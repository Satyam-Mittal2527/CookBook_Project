Pumpkin Bean Spicy!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups pumpkin, diced
  • 1 cup black beans (cooked or canned, rinsed and drained)
  • 1 cup kidney beans (cooked or canned, rinsed and drained)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

1
Peel and dice the pumpkin.
2
Rinse and drain the black beans and kidney beans.
3
Finely chop the onion and mince the garlic.
4
Dice the red bell pepper and tomatoes.
5
Sauté the Vegetables and simultaneouly heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
6
Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, sauté until translucent and fragrant (about 3-4 minutes)
7
Add the Spices: cumin powder, chili powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper to the pot.
8
Stir well to coat the onion and garlic with the spices.
9
Cook the Vegetables and Beans.
10
Add diced pumpkin, bell pepper, and tomatoes to the pot.
11
Cook for about 5 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
12
Add the black beans and kidney beans to the pot.
13
Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
14
Simmer the Stew: Reduce the heat to low and let the stew simmer for about 20-25 minutes until the pumpkin is tender and the flavors are well combined.
15
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
16
Garnish and Serve.
