Nacho Chicken Salad!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 can (400 grams) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, canned, or frozen)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag tortilla chips, crushed

Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1
In a large mixing bowl, combine shredded chicken, black beans, corn kernels, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, red onion, cherry tomatoes, avocado, and fresh cilantro.
2
In a separate small bowl, whisk together sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, chili powder, cumin powder, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
3
Pour the dressing over the chicken and vegetable mixture and toss to coat evenly.
4
Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese and crushed tortilla chips over the salad.
5
Garnish with additional fresh cilantro if desired and serve immediately.
