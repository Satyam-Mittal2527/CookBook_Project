Fajitas Fiesta!

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced into strips
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Flour tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Sour cream, guacamole, and salsa for serving

Instructions:

1
Slice the chicken breasts, bell peppers, and onion.
2
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3
Add the sliced chicken strips and cook until browned and cooked through.
4
Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
5
In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers and onion.
6
Cook the vegetables until they are tender and slightly charred.
7
Add the minced garlic and saut√© for another minute.
8
Return the cooked chicken strips to the skillet.
9
Add the chili powder, cumin powder, paprika, oregano, black pepper, and salt. Stir well to combine.
10
Cook for another 2-3 minutes, ensuring the chicken and vegetables are well coated with the spices.
11
Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
12
Serve the fajita mixture on the warm tortillas.
13
Garnish with fresh cilantro.
14
Serve with sour cream, guacamole, and salsa.
