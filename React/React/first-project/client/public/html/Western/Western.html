Western Cuisine Recipes

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and mix with grated Parmesan.
  4. Combine spaghetti with pancetta and remove from heat.
  5. Quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef
  • 1 onion
  • 200g mushrooms
  • 200ml sour cream
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Slice beef into strips and season with salt and pepper.
  2. In a pan, cook beef until browned, then remove from pan.
  3. Add sliced onion and mushrooms to the pan and sauté until soft.
  4. Return beef to the pan and stir in sour cream.
  5. Simmer for a few minutes until heated through.

Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 200g fettuccine
  • 200g chicken breast
  • 100ml cream
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions.
  2. Season chicken breast with salt and pepper and cook in a pan until golden.
  3. Remove chicken and slice it.
  4. In the same pan, add cream and Parmesan cheese, stirring until combined.
  5. Add cooked fettuccine and sliced chicken to the sauce, mixing well.

Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 8 taco shells
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Chopped tomatoes

Instructions:

  1. Cook ground beef in a pan until browned.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water, simmering for 5 minutes.
  3. Fill taco shells with beef mixture and top with lettuce and tomatoes.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and grill the sandwich until golden brown on both sides.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • Caesar dressing

Instructions:

  1. Chop romaine lettuce and place in a bowl.
  2. Add croutons and shaved Parmesan cheese.
  3. Drizzle with Caesar dressing and toss to combine.

Meatloaf

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 egg
  • 100g breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. In a bowl, mix ground beef, chopped onion, egg, breadcrumbs, salt, and pepper.
  3. Shape mixture into a loaf and place in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 1 hour or until cooked through.

Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 pie crust
  • 300g cooked chicken
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. In a bowl, combine chicken, vegetables, and broth.
  3. Pour mixture into pie crust and cover with another crust.
  4. Cut slits in the top crust and bake for 30-35 minutes.

Clam Chowder

Ingredients:

  • 500g clams
  • 1 onion
  • 2 potatoes
  • 500ml cream
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Chop onion and potatoes.
  2. In a pot, cook onion and potatoes until soft.
  3. Add clams and cream, simmering for 10 minutes.

BBQ Ribs

Ingredients:

  • 1kg pork ribs
  • BBQ sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C.
  2. Season ribs with salt and pepper.
  3. Bake ribs for 2 hours, brushing with BBQ sauce halfway through.

Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 200g macaroni
  • 100g cheese
  • 50ml milk
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Cook macaroni according to package instructions.
  2. In a pot, melt butter and add milk and cheese.
  3. Mix in cooked macaroni until well combined.

Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground lamb
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • Mashed potatoes

Instructions:

  1. Cook ground lamb and chopped onion until browned.
  2. Add diced carrots and cook until tender.
  3. Transfer mixture to a baking dish and top with mashed potatoes.
  4. Bake at 200°C for 30 minutes.

Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 100g breadcrumbs
  • 100g mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Coat chicken breasts in breadcrumbs.
  3. Fry chicken until golden, then place in a baking dish.
  4. Top with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese, then bake for 20 minutes.

Fish and Chips

Ingredients:

  • 2 fish fillets
  • 200g potatoes
  • Flour
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into chips, then fry until golden.
  2. Coat fish fillets in flour and fry until crispy.
  3. Serve with chips and tartar sauce.

Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 200g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, eggs, and milk until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan and pour in batter to form pancakes.
  3. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.

Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 200g lasagna sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g tomato sauce
  • 100g cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Layer lasagna sheets, ground beef, tomato sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  3. Bake for 45 minutes until golden and bubbly.

Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers
  • 300g ground beef
  • 100g rice
  • Tomato sauce

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Cook rice according to package instructions.
  3. Mix cooked rice with ground beef and tomato sauce.
  4. Stuff mixture into halved bell peppers and bake for 30 minutes.

Baked Ziti

Ingredients:

  • 200g ziti pasta
  • 400g marinara sauce
  • 100g mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook ziti according to package instructions.
  2. Mix cooked ziti with marinara sauce and half of the cheese.
  3. Transfer to a baking dish, top with remaining cheese, and bake for 20 minutes.

Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 200g fettuccine
  • 100ml cream
  • 50g Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions.
  2. In a saucepan, heat cream and stir in Parmesan cheese until melted.
  3. Toss cooked fettuccine with the sauce and serve.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 200g flour
  • 100g sugar
  • 100g butter
  • 100g chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. In a bowl, cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Add flour and chocolate chips, mixing until combined.
  4. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
